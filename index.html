    Services

    Dog Behaviour House Visit Assessment

    The purpose of this assessment is to evaluate your dog’s behaviour, temperament, interactions between you and your dog, and living environment to help the owner discover current situations, planning training details and directions.

    Puppy Training and Development

    A program for new puppy Owner or first time puppy owner, puppy training includes toilet potty training, house boundaries, outdoor public learning, basic commands and safety prevention. Help to raise a confident and quality puppy, owner learn to manage their puppy in simple and effective ways.

    Behavior Modification

    To address dog which 6 months old and above that has anxiety, stress, problem solving that those behaviours and foundation passed by puppy stage and didn’t manage to learn and didn’t develop well. Owner able to learn to condition and manage those behaviours and maintain in daily basis.

    Aggression Rehabilitation

    For Dog who attacked and bite ppl and other dogs or other animals, this program helps to identify the behaviours, learn to setup safety precautions, and support to reduce and prevent bite from happening.

    Board and Learn

    Dog boarding over night at trainer place for several weeks for trainer to lay foundation for the owner weekly owner come to practice and after foundation done, owner follow up training after the dog gone home.

    Private One on One

    Trainer provide several lessons of training with owner training dog together at owner house or any location for reality practica purpose.

    External Unburdening Consultation (Human only)

    Consultation for people who want to explore inner needs and unburden whichever emotional, mind state and body memories not belong to self.

    Aura Healing Energy Touch (Dog only)

    Provide Aura Healing Energy towards dog to support body or emotions comfort. Consult needed before perform
